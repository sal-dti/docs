[See detailed API reference](/api-reference/video/get-video)

After you've created your video, you have the option to monitor its progress. Once the video is fully processed,
its status will update to `completed`.

<Note>
  Alternatively, you can [configure the webhook](#configuring-webhook-optional)
  to be notified when the status of the video changes.
</Note>

<CodeGroup>

```bash Request
curl --request GET
--url 'https://api.deepreel.com/project/assets/video?id=<video_id>'
--header 'x-api-key: <your-api-key>'
```
